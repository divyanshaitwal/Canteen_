🚀 Getting Started

New to CanteenGo? Start Here

📝

How do I sign up for CanteenGo?

Signing up is easy! Download the CanteenGo app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Open the app and click "Sign Up" on the welcome screen. Enter your name, email, phone number, and create a password. Verify your phone number with the OTP code sent to you. Complete your profile by adding your campus details and delivery address. That's it! You're ready to start ordering delicious food from your favorite campus canteens.

🍽️

How do I place my first order?

Placing your first order is simple: 1) Browse restaurants and menus by tapping "Explore" on the home screen, 2) Select items you want and add them to your cart, 3) Review your cart and apply any promo codes, 4) Choose your delivery location (dorm, library, classroom), 5) Select payment method (card, campus wallet, or cash), 6) Confirm and place your order. You'll receive real-time updates and can track your delivery live on the map!

📱

What features does the app offer?

CanteenGo offers comprehensive features: Real-time order tracking with live map, schedule orders in advance for busy days, browse menus with photos and ratings, secure payments with multiple options, loyalty rewards program with points and discounts, saved addresses and favorite orders for quick reordering, dietary filters (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free), group ordering for study sessions, order history and reorder functionality, 24/7 customer support chat, and exclusive student discounts!

🏫

Which campuses are available?

CanteenGo currently serves 50+ college campuses across major cities. We're expanding rapidly! Check the app's "Location" section to see if your campus is covered. If not, you can submit a request to bring CanteenGo to your campus. We partner with campus canteens, food courts, and nearby restaurants to bring you maximum variety and convenience.

📦 Orders & Delivery

Everything About Your Orders

📍

How do I track my order?

Tracking is built right into the app! Go to "My Orders" from the bottom menu and select your active order. You'll see live updates: Order Confirmed → Preparing → Out for Delivery → Delivered. The map shows your delivery partner's real-time location. You'll get push notifications at each stage. Estimated delivery time updates automatically based on preparation time and distance.

⏱️

How long does delivery take?

Average delivery time is 15-30 minutes depending on: restaurant preparation time (5-15 mins), distance to your location (within campus usually 5-15 mins), and current order volume (peak lunch/dinner times may take longer). You'll see estimated delivery time before placing your order. We aim for fast service while ensuring your food arrives hot and fresh!

❌

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Yes, but timing matters: Before restaurant accepts (1-2 mins): Full cancellation available with instant refund. After restaurant starts preparing: Contact support immediately - we'll try to modify if possible. Order already out for delivery: Cancellation not available, but you can refuse delivery for valid reasons (wrong items, quality issues). Refunds process within 3-5 business days to your original payment method.

📅

Can I schedule orders in advance?

Absolutely! Schedule orders up to 7 days in advance. Perfect for busy class schedules! When placing an order, tap "Schedule for Later" and choose your preferred date and time slot. Your order will be prepared fresh and delivered at the scheduled time. Great for lunch breaks, study sessions, or late-night snacks during exam week!

💰

Is there a minimum order value?

Minimum order value varies by restaurant (typically $5-10). Delivery fee is usually $1-3 for on-campus delivery. Orders over $15 often qualify for free delivery! Check the restaurant page for specific details. Pro tip: Use group ordering feature to split costs with friends and meet minimums easily!

🔄

What if my order is wrong or incomplete?

We apologize for any issues! If you receive the wrong order or missing items: 1) Don't start eating - take photos of what you received, 2) Open the app and tap "Help" button on your order, 3) Select "Wrong/Missing Items" and upload photos, 4) Our support team responds within 5 minutes, 5) We'll either send missing items or issue a full refund. Your satisfaction is our priority!

💳 Payments & Refunds

Payment Methods and Billing Help

💰

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept multiple payment options: Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), Campus Meal Plans & Wallet Credits, Digital Wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal), UPI Payments (for supported regions), Cash on Delivery (available for select orders), and Gift Cards & Promo Codes. All online payments are secured with 256-bit encryption. Choose your preferred method at checkout!

🔙

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are processed for: Order cancelled before preparation (100% refund), wrong items delivered (full or partial refund), food quality issues with photo proof (full refund), excessive delivery delay (compensation or refund), restaurant cancellation (automatic full refund). Refunds typically take 3-5 business days to reflect in your account. For immediate credit, we can add to your CanteenGo wallet for your next order!

🎟️

How do I use promo codes?

Applying promo codes is easy: Add items to cart and proceed to checkout, look for "Apply Promo Code" field, enter your code and tap "Apply", discount will be automatically calculated, complete your order. Check "Offers" section in the app for active deals! New users get FIRST20 for 20% off. Follow us on social media for exclusive codes!

🧾

Can I get a receipt for my order?

Yes! Digital receipts are automatically generated for every order. To access: Go to "My Orders" → Select completed order → Tap "View Receipt" → Download as PDF or email to yourself. Receipts include item-wise breakdown, taxes & fees, payment method used, date & time, and order ID for reference. Perfect for expense tracking or reimbursements!

👤 Account Settings

Manage Your Profile and Preferences

🔐

How do I change my password?

To change password: Tap "Profile" → "Settings" → "Security" → "Change Password". Enter current password, then new password twice. If you forgot your password: Tap "Forgot Password" on login screen → Enter registered email/phone → Receive OTP code → Create new password. For security, use a strong password with letters, numbers, and symbols!

✏️

How do I update my profile information?

Update your details anytime: Go to "Profile" → Tap "Edit Profile" → Modify name, email, phone, or profile picture → Save changes. To add/edit delivery addresses: "Profile" → "Saved Addresses" → Add New or Edit existing. You can save multiple addresses (dorm, class buildings, library) for quick selection during checkout!

🗑️

Can I delete my account?

We're sorry to see you go! To delete: Go to "Profile" → "Settings" → "Account" → "Delete Account" → Confirm deletion. Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All order history, saved addresses, and loyalty points will be lost. Active orders must be completed first. Consider temporarily deactivating instead if you might return!

🔔

How do I manage notifications?

Customize your notification preferences: "Profile" → "Settings" → "Notifications". Choose what you want to receive: Order updates (recommended to keep ON), promotional offers, new restaurant openings, delivery partner location updates, payment confirmations, loyalty rewards alerts. You can enable push notifications, email notifications, or SMS alerts individually. Never miss important order updates!

🔧 Technical Support

App Issues and Technical Help

⚠️

The app is not working properly. What should I do?

Try these quick fixes: 1) Close and restart the app, 2) Check your internet connection (WiFi or mobile data), 3) Clear app cache: Settings → Apps → CanteenGo → Clear Cache, 4) Update to latest version from app store, 5) Restart your phone, 6) Uninstall and reinstall app (your account data is saved). Still not working? Contact support with your device model and issue description for personalized help!

❗

My payment failed. What went wrong?

Common payment failure reasons: Insufficient balance in account, incorrect card details or expired card, bank blocking online transactions (enable e-commerce), network interruption during payment, daily transaction limit reached, or 3D Secure authentication timeout. If money was deducted but order failed, don't worry - it will be auto-refunded within 5-7 days. Contact support with transaction ID for faster resolution!

📍

Location/GPS not working correctly?

Fix location issues: Enable Location Services: Phone Settings → Location → ON, Grant app permission: Settings → Apps → CanteenGo → Permissions → Location → Always Allow, ensure GPS is set to "High Accuracy" mode, move to an open area if indoors (GPS works best outdoors), or manually enter your delivery address if GPS fails. You can save multiple addresses to avoid repeated GPS usage!

🐌

Why is the app running slow?

Speed up your app: Clear cache regularly (Settings → Storage → Clear Cache), close background apps consuming RAM, ensure stable internet (try WiFi over mobile data), update to latest app version (optimizations included), free up phone storage (app needs 50MB+ free space), or restart your device. If still slow, check if your device meets minimum requirements (Android 7.0+ / iOS 12.0+).

🛡️ Safety & Quality

Food Safety and Quality Standards

🛡️

How do you ensure food safety?

Food safety is our top priority: All partner restaurants undergo strict hygiene audits, regular kitchen inspections by certified food safety officers, temperature-controlled delivery bags to maintain food freshness, contactless delivery options available, packaging sealed at restaurant (tampering evident), delivery partners follow hygiene protocols (masks, sanitization), and real-time quality monitoring system. We comply with all local health department regulations!

🥗

Can I filter for dietary restrictions?

Yes! We support comprehensive dietary filters: Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free, Nut-Free, Halal, Kosher, and Low-Carb options. Set preferences in "Profile" → "Dietary Preferences" for personalized recommendations. Each menu item shows ingredients and allergen information. However, always verify with restaurant for severe allergies as cross-contamination may occur in kitchens.

😞

What if I'm not satisfied with food quality?

We value your feedback! If food quality is below expectations: Take photos of the issue, open your order and tap "Report Issue", select "Food Quality Problem" (taste, temperature, freshness, etc.), describe the issue and upload photos, and our support team will review within 10 minutes. You'll receive appropriate compensation (refund, credits, or replacement). We share feedback with restaurants to maintain high standards!

🚪

Do you offer contactless delivery?

Yes! Contactless delivery is available for all orders. How it works: Select "Contactless Delivery" at checkout, add specific drop-off instructions (door number, landmark), delivery partner will place order at your specified location, you'll receive notification when order is delivered, and pick up your order safely. Payment must be online for contactless delivery. This option ensures everyone's safety while maintaining convenience!
