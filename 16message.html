💳 Payment Information

Information about payment methods and security

💰 What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept multiple payment methods: Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), Digital Wallets (eSewa, Khalti, IME Pay), Mobile Banking, Cash on Delivery, and Campus ID Card (for registered students). All digital payments are processed securely with 256-bit encryption.

🔒 Is my payment information secure? +

Absolutely! We use PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways with end-to-end encryption. Your card details are never stored on our servers. We use tokenization for recurring payments and two-factor authentication for transactions over Rs. 5,000. Our security is audited quarterly by independent cybersecurity firms.

💵 Can I split payment between multiple methods? +

Yes! You can split payments using: (1) Wallet + Card, (2) Campus ID + Cash, (3) Multiple digital wallets. Just select "Split Payment" at checkout. Example: Use Rs. 200 from eSewa wallet and pay the rest via card. Maximum 2 payment methods per order.

⏱️ How long does payment processing take? +

Instant: Digital wallets (eSewa, Khalti) - confirmed in 2-5 seconds. Cards: 10-30 seconds. Mobile Banking: 1-2 minutes. Cash on Delivery: Payment upon receiving your order. You'll receive instant confirmation via SMS and app notification once payment clears.

🎫 Do you offer student discounts or vouchers? +

Yes! Student Verified Accounts: 10% off on first 3 orders monthly. Loyalty Points: Earn 1 point per Rs. 100 spent (1 point = Rs. 1). Referral Bonus: Rs. 100 off for both you and your friend. Festival Offers: Up to 50% off during Dashain, Tihar, New Year. Check the "Offers" tab in-app for active promotions!

🔄 What's your refund policy? +

Full Refund: Order cancelled before restaurant confirmation (instant). Partial Refund: Wrong/damaged items (processed within 24 hours). Store Credit: If you prefer, get 110% as wallet credit. Processing Time: Refunds to original payment method take 5-7 business days. Wallet refunds are instant. No questions asked policy!

🚚 Delivery Information

Delivery times, tracking and logistics

⏰ How long does delivery take? +

Express Delivery: 15-20 minutes (peak hours: 20-30 min). Standard Delivery: 30-45 minutes. Schedule Later: Up to 24 hours in advance. Average campus delivery: 18 minutes! Track real-time through our app with live GPS. ETA updates every 2 minutes. Rush hours: 12PM-2PM, 6PM-8PM may add 5-10 minutes.

📍 Do you deliver to all campus locations? +

Yes! We deliver to all Techspire College buildings: Hostels (A, B, C, D), Academic Blocks (Main, Engineering, Science), Library, Sports Complex, Cafeteria, Admin Block, Faculty Housing, and Parking Areas. Specify exact location (building + floor/room) at checkout. Outdoor delivery available at Basketball Court, Football Field, Central Lawn during events!

🔍 How do I track my order? +

Real-Time Tracking: Open app → "My Orders" → Select active order → Live GPS map shows delivery partner location. SMS Updates: Order confirmed, Preparing, Picked up, Out for delivery, Delivered. Notifications: Push alerts at each stage. Direct Call: Contact delivery partner directly via app (number masked for privacy). No tracking? Call support immediately!

📦 What if my order arrives late or wrong? +

Late Delivery: Orders >45 min late get automatic Rs. 50 credit. Report via "Order Issue" button. Wrong Order: Keep original, we'll send correct items free + full refund. Missing Items: Instant refund or redelivery. Cold/Stale Food: Full refund + Rs. 100 apology credit. Report within 15 minutes of delivery for fastest resolution!

🚫 Can I cancel or modify my order after placing it? +

Free Cancellation: Before restaurant accepts (usually 2-3 min) - instant full refund. After Acceptance: Cancellation fee Rs. 20-50 depending on prep stage. Modify Order: Call restaurant directly (number in order details) within 5 minutes. After Dispatch: Cannot cancel, but you can reject at delivery (re-stocking fee may apply). Cancel via app or call +977 9876543210 ext. 101.

💸 Are there delivery charges? +

On-Campus: FREE for orders above Rs. 200. Below Rs. 200: Rs. 30 delivery fee. Peak Hours: No surge pricing ever! Group Orders: Free delivery regardless of amount when 3+ people order together. Express Delivery: +Rs. 20 for 15-min guarantee. Premium Members: FREE delivery on all orders + priority dispatch!

👤 Account Management

Managing your account and orders

📝 How do I create an account? +

3 Easy Ways: (1) Student Email: Use your .edu email for instant student verification. (2) Mobile Number: OTP-based signup in 30 seconds. (3) Social Login: Google/Facebook single-click signup. Fill in basic details (name, campus address) and start ordering! New users get Rs. 100 welcome credit automatically added to wallet.

🔐 How do I reset my password? +

Click "Forgot Password" on login screen → Enter registered email/mobile → Receive OTP/reset link → Create new password (min 8 characters, 1 uppercase, 1 number). Alternative: Email support@canteengo.com with registered email. Security: We'll verify your identity via registered phone for security. Password reset links expire in 15 minutes.

🎯 What is the loyalty program? +

CanteenGo Points: Earn 1 point per Rs. 100 spent (1 point = Rs. 1). Tiers: Bronze (0-499 pts), Silver (500-999), Gold (1000+) with increasing benefits. Gold Perks: 2x points, free delivery, birthday meal, early access to offers, priority support. Redeem: Minimum 100 points. Referrals: 100 bonus points per friend. Points never expire!

📧 How do I update my profile information? +

Go to Profile → Edit Details. Update: Name, Email (requires verification), Phone (OTP required), Profile Picture, Default Address, Dietary Preferences (veg/non-veg/vegan), Allergen Warnings. Saved Addresses: Add multiple delivery locations. Privacy: Control what delivery partners see. Changes save automatically. Email change requires re-verification within 24 hours.

📜 Where can I view my order history? +

App/Website: Menu → "My Orders" → Filter by: All, Delivered, Cancelled, Scheduled. Details Shown: Order date/time, items, restaurant, amount, delivery person, rating. Actions: Reorder with 1-click, Download invoice, Report issue, Rate order. Export: Download complete order history as PDF for expense tracking. Search: Find orders by restaurant name, dish, or date range.

🗑️ How do I delete my account? +

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Before Deletion: Complete pending orders, withdraw wallet balance, cancel subscriptions. Process: 7-day grace period (can restore anytime). What's Deleted: Profile, order history, saved cards, addresses. What's Retained: Financial records (legal requirement). Alternative: Deactivate temporarily without deletion. Email support@canteengo.com for assistance.
