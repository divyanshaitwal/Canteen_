FAQ

Common questions about ordering and delivery.

🕐 What are your operating hours?

We're open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Sunday hours are 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM.

🚚 How long does delivery take?

Average delivery time is 20-30 minutes depending on your location. We strive for fast service!

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, mobile wallets (eSewa, Khalti), and UPI payments.

📦 Can I customize my order?

Yes! Most dishes can be customized. Add special instructions in the order notes section.

🔄 What's your cancellation policy?

Orders can be cancelled within 2 minutes of placement for a full refund. After that, no refunds.

🌿 Do you have vegetarian options?

Absolutely! We have a wide variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options clearly marked.
