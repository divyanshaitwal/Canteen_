📖 Our Journey

Our Story

CanteenGo was founded in 2020 with a bold vision: to connect hungry people with delicious meals from their favorite local restaurants. What started as a simple idea among three college friends has blossomed into a trusted platform serving millions of customers across thousands of restaurant partners.

We recognized the challenges students and busy professionals face when trying to grab a quick, quality meal. Long queues, limited options, and inconsistent service were daily frustrations. So we built CanteenGo—a seamless bridge between you and the food you love.

Today, we're proud to partner with restaurants ranging from local campus favorites to established chains, bringing diverse cuisines right to your doorstep. Every order placed through CanteenGo supports local businesses and helps build stronger food communities.

📅 Milestones

Our History

From a college dorm idea to serving millions—here's how we grew.

2020

🚀 The Beginning

Founded by three college friends frustrated with campus dining options. Launched beta version serving 5 campus restaurants.

2021

📈 Rapid Growth

Expanded to 10 campuses, partnered with 200+ restaurants. Achieved 50,000+ happy customers and raised seed funding.

2022

🏆 Major Milestone

Crossed 1 million orders delivered. Launched mobile app for iOS and Android. Introduced subscription plans for students.

2023

🌍 National Expansion

Expanded to 50+ cities nationwide. Partnered with 5,000+ restaurants. Introduced real-time tracking and AI-powered recommendations.

2024

💡 Innovation Era

Launched smart canteen kiosks, contactless pickup, and eco-friendly packaging initiative. Won "Best Food-Tech Startup" award.

2025-26

🎯 Future Ready

Serving 1M+ customers monthly. Building AI chatbot for instant support, exploring drone delivery, and expanding to international markets.

Our Values

The core principles that guide everything we do at CanteenGo.

⚡

Speed & Efficiency

We know you're hungry. That's why we optimize every step—from order placement to delivery—ensuring you get your meal fast without compromising quality.

🎯

Quality First

We partner only with restaurants that meet our strict quality standards. Every meal delivered represents our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.

🤝

Community Focus

We're more than a delivery service—we're building food communities. Supporting local restaurants, creating jobs, and bringing people together over great meals.

💚

Sustainability

Environmental responsibility matters. We use eco-friendly packaging, optimize delivery routes to reduce emissions, and partner with green restaurants.

🔒

Trust & Safety

Your safety is paramount. We verify all restaurant partners, ensure food hygiene standards, and provide secure payment options with transparent pricing.

🌟

Customer Delight

Your happiness drives us. From 24/7 support to personalized recommendations, we go the extra mile to ensure every CanteenGo experience exceeds expectations.
